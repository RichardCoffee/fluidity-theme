

		Document Conventions
	--------------------------

	The key words “MUST”, “MUST NOT”, “REQUIRED”, “SHALL”, “SHALL NOT”,
		“SHOULD”, “SHOULD NOT”, “RECOMMENDED”, “MAY”, and “OPTIONAL” in
		this document are to be interpreted as described in
		<a href="http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc2119" target="rfc_2119">RFC 2119</a>.

	When writing text for this document, please consider using
		<a href="http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc2360" target="rfc_2360">RFC 2360</a>
		as a guideline.



		Bones of Contention Agreement
	-----------------------------------

	Anyone MAY add a Bone to the contention list.

	Only the person who added the Bone SHALL remove the Bone.

	Bones SHOULD NOT not be worked on by anyone, until an agreement is reached
		on how progress on the Bone is to be achieved.



		Budget Agreement
	----------------------

	Company funds MUST be deposited, without alteration, into a previously
		determined holding account, or location, upon receipt.

	Company funds SHALL NOT be dispersed, spent, committed, or handled in
		any way, without prior agreement by all parties concerned.

	Refusal of funds dissemination by any party MUST be respected.

	Dispersal of profits to any party MUST coincide with equal dispersements
		to all parties concerned.  If disagreements occur over the amount of
		the dispersement, then either the lower amount MUST be used or the issue
		MUST be resolved via the 'Bones of Contention' mechanism.

	Violation of any item contained within the 'Budget Agreement' MUST result
		in complete termination of all agreements laid out in this document.
