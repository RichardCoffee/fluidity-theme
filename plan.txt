
	Document Conventions
--------------------------

	No line longer than 80 characters.  This is to facilitate printing.
		It doesn't matter if the document is never actually printed.



	Bones of Contention
------------------------

	Anyone can add a bone to the contention list.

	Only the person who added the bone can remove the bone.

	Bones can not be worked on by anyone, until an agreement is reached on
		how work on the bone is to proceed.
